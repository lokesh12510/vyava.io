About Us Something about us

Vyava is an important business committed to the manufacture and sale of a wide variety of Indian spices. The key empowerment lies in the millions of smallholder producers and landless workers who form the backbone of rural economies in most developing countries to grow their incomes and improve their livelihoods by raising agricultural productivity and engaging in markets. Whole Spices, Ground Spices, Blended Spices, Curry Powders and Baking Spices are part of the offered product selection. With the assistance of high quality machines, our products are prepared and are in high demand by our customers for their precise composition, non-toxic nature and high purity.

We have a team of seasoned professionals with rich experience and academic skills. Our experts, chosen by an in-depth recruiting process on the basis of their industrial expertise and experience, are always ready to meet the industry's requirements. In addition, our experts have dedicated themselves to meeting the exact requirements of our customers in the most appropriate manner.

India has an ideal climate for growing spices which is why it is the largest producer of spices in the world. Different kinds of spices grow in each region of India due to different climatic conditions across the country. Out of the 109 spices listed with International Organization for Standardization(ISO), 75 types of spices are produced in India. Indian states Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Kerala, Gujrat, Karnataka, Maharashtra, Rajasthan, Tamil Nadu etc. contribute majorly to the spice production in India.
